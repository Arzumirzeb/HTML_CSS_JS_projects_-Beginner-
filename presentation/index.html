How we are working

Algorithm for working with us for convenience and understanding of the project

  • Idea

    A client comes to us with an idea. This could be an image/sketch or other reference. And we think about how to implement this, make technical drawings and offer solutions on materials.

  • Technical task

    Together with the client, we formulate the correct technical specification, which includes visualization of the product, a sketch drawing with dimensions, information on materials and finishing, project implementation period and other mandatory items.

  • Commercial offer

    We preferably use natural materials. But we can optimize any estimate by simplifying the materials or design. We will be able to adapt to the clientâ€™s budget and agree on a commercial proposal

  • Preparing a working draft

    We will create working documentation and drawings. This is the foundation of quality production. At this stage, we approve all dimensions and materials, drawings with the client and begin production.

  • Production and installation

    Production takes from 15 working days, depending on complexity and volume. We also do the installation ourselves. After all, we will do it quickly and accurately.
